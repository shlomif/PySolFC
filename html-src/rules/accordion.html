Accordion

One-Deck game type. 1 deck. No redeal.

Object

Remove all cards but one to a single pile.

Rules

Cards are dealt from the stock in a sequence. If two cards of the same suit or rank are next to each other, or separated by exactly two cards, the card that is further to the left (in PySol, this is the card that is further from the deck) may be moved on top of the other.

The game is won when only one card remains.

Notes

Accordion, and its related games, can be played with cards dealt manually by the player one at a time, or all cards dealt at once. This option can be set in the options menu.
