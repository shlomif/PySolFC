Agnes Bernauer

Raglan type. 1 deck. No redeal.

Object

Move all cards to the foundations.

Rules

Cards are dealt face-up in seven piles of ascending length. Seven cards are set aside to be reserve piles and one card is dealt to a foundation. This card determines the base rank of the foundations.

Cards in the Tableau are built down by alternate color. Groups of cards in sequence can be moved together.

Each deal flips one card from the Stock to the top of each of the reserve piles. There is no redeal.

Foundations are built up in suit in sequence, wrapping from King to Ace when necessary. Cards in Foundations are still in play. Double clicking on a card in the Tableau will move it to the appropriate Foundation pile if such a move is possible.
