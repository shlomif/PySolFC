Deuces and Queens

Two-Deck game type. 2 decks. No redeal.

Object

Move all the cards to the foundations.

Quick Description

Like Aces and Kings, but with three reserves, and one set of foundations builds up from two to ace, and the other builds down from queen to king, both wrapping between king and ace as necessary.

Additionally, building on the tableau piles is allowed. Tableau piles are built up or down by rank, regardless of suit, also wrapping between king and ace as necessary. But once the talon is empty, empty tableau piles can no longer be filled.
