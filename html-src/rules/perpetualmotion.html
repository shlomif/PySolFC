Perpetual Motion

One-Deck game type. 1 deck. Unlimited redeals.

Object

Move all cards to the single foundation.

Rules

Cards are dealt from the talon to the tableau four at a time. The tableau piles build by cards of same rank.

A pile of four cards of the same rank can be moved to the single foundation.

After all cards have been dealt click on the talon for a redeal. The cards are not re-shuffled, but re-dealt in a certain pattern. The piles are stacked together, with the rightmost pile flipped and placed on the bottom, and each of the other piles flipped and placed on top, going from right to left.

The game is won if all cards are moved to the foundation.

Strategy

Good for mindless playing as sooner or later every game should come out.
